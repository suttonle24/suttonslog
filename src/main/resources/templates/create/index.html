    Uploading and inserting images...
  1. Drag and drop your image into the box to the left, or click browse to select your images.
    (You can upload multiple images at once)
  2. Insert an image into your post with one of two methods: You can click 'Insert image' inside of the 'Insert' dropdown. You can also click on the 'Insert/edit image' button on the far-right of the text editor.
  3. When you insert an image, a dialog box will pop up. It is important that the Source field looks like this: /blogPhotos/YOUR_IMAGE_HERE. So, if you uploaded an image called house.jpg the source field should look like this: /blogPhotos/house.jpg.
